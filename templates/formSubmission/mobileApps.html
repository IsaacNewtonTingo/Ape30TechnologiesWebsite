"My Doctor" Mobile Application

This is a self-diagnosis mobile application. Users enter their signs and symptoms and in return, the application displays their potential ailment. Additionally, users can search for nearest ambulances and health facilities. During signup, users add next of kin contact details that they are able to cantact during and emergency. Their is also a records section where users can record the hospitals visited and ambulance services used.

This is a church mobile application. Members are able to create an acoount and interact with the application. There is section where they get "Today's reading" which is posted by the admin.
